Who We Are

Founded in the United States. Built for the World.

American Allied University was incorporated in Minnesota in 2024, founded by an entrepreneur and educator committed to expanding access to quality education for working professionals and global learners everywhere.

AAU currently delivers professional certificate programs through our dedicated Coursera Classroom for Organizations — a curated, structured learning environment where students are enrolled directly into tailored program paths.

Graduate degree programs including the MBA, M.Ed., and MOL are in development for a future launch date.

"We believe quality education should be accessible regardless of geography, schedule, or circumstance."
